About Greenroom Electrical

Local, reliable electrical services with a focus on safety and quality workmanship.

Greenroom Electrical is a locally owned business based in the Clarence Valley, providing residential and light commercial electrical services. From small repairs to larger upgrades, every job is completed with care, clear communication, and attention to detail.

Whether you need new lighting, safety switch checks, smoke alarm upgrades, or help troubleshooting an electrical fault, you’ll get straightforward advice and tidy, professional work. The goal is simple: make your home or workplace safer, more efficient, and easier to live and work in.

If you’d like to discuss a project or organise a quote, get in touch and we’ll find a time that suits you.
